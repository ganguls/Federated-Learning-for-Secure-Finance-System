Research Overview

This demonstration showcases our Local Differential Privacy (LDP) + K-means clustering approach for detecting malicious clients in federated learning systems. The system preserves client privacy while effectively identifying various data poisoning attacks.

Key Features: Real-time attack detection, privacy-preserving analysis, multiple attack scenario support, and comprehensive performance metrics.
